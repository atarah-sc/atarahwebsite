About

What is Atarah?

Atarah — the Hebrew word for Crown — is a dynamic inter-church event hosted by the CSI Shanthi Church Youth Fellowship, bringing together youth aged 16–35 from congregations across Bangalore.

Taking place at Cathedral High School on 11th July 2026, Atarah is a platform where faith meets creativity — where every short film, photograph, performance, and spoken word carries the mark of the King.

"Crowned With Purpose — an invitation to youth to discover, celebrate and express their identity in Christ."

With 10 diverse competitions spanning film, music, spoken word, visual arts, and biblical knowledge, Atarah creates a memorable, faith-filled experience for all who participate. Each event carries Brownie Points contributing to the overall church championship score.

General Rules & Regulations

  • Participants must be between age 16 and 35 years.
  • Entry forms must be submitted duly attested by the Presbyter-in-Charge & Youth Representative on the day of the event.
  • Registration deadline: 8th July 2026.
  • Participation in each event carries Brownie Points for the overall scores.
  • A cash prize of ₹10,000/- along with Winners & Rolling Trophies will be awarded to the overall champions of ATARAH 2026.
  • Organisers are not responsible for any personal loss or damages during the event.
  • Participants must carry their respective ID proof.
  • Registration desk opens at 9:00 AM — adhere to the event schedule.
  • Participants are requested to follow decorum and respect the school campus.
  • Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited.

Central Theme

The Heart of Atarah

✦   Hebrew: עֲטָרָה   ✦
Crowned With Purpose

"You will be a crown of splendour in the Lord's hand,
a royal diadem in the hand of your God."

— Isaiah 62:3

Atarah — the Hebrew word for Crown — is more than a competition title. It is an identity declaration. Every event, every performance, every creative work is an act of worship and testimony, rooted in the truth that those who follow Christ are Crowned With Purpose. Participants are invited to infuse their talents with this conviction, creating work that is both artistically excellent and spiritually meaningful.
